Prem Oracle — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Prem Oracle is a Premier League score-prediction app operated by AB80 Ltd ("we", "us"). We designed it to collect as little data as possible. This page explains exactly what is stored and why.

What we collect

We do not ask for, and never collect, your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or photos. There are no accounts and no passwords. The only data the app stores is:

A random player ID. Generated on your device the first time you play. It is a random string and contains nothing about you.

Your display name. Whatever nickname you choose to show in league tables.

Your predictions. The scores you pick, and when you picked them, so leagues can be scored fairly.

Leagues. The names and membership of leagues you create or join.

A push notification token (optional). Only if you enable kick-off reminders; used solely to deliver those notifications. Declining notifications does not affect the app.

Where it is stored

Your picks and leagues are stored on your device and, so that leagues work across devices, on our server infrastructure provided by Cloudflare, Inc. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell or share your data. We show no advertising and use no advertising or tracking SDKs. We use no third-party analytics. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

League visibility

Members of a league you join can see your display name, your points, and — after each match kicks off — the prediction you made for that match. Predictions are hidden from other players before kick-off.

Data retention and deletion

Data is kept while the season's game is running. To delete your data, contact us with your three-word recovery code (shown in the League tab) and we will remove your player record, picks, and league memberships. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

Children

Prem Oracle is suitable for all ages. We collect no personal information from anyone, including children.
